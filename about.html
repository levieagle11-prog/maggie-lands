About Maggie

Hello, friend — I'm glad you're here.

"She is clothed with strength and dignity, and she laughs without fear of the future." — Proverbs 31:25

I'm Maggie — a twenty-something writer, daughter of the King, and lover of slow mornings, strong tea, and the kind of books that leave ink on your fingers and light in your chest. Maggie Lands is the little corner of the internet where I write down the things I'm learning, feeling, and praying through.

I started this blog because I kept finding myself scribbling in journals and thinking: maybe someone else needs to hear this too. Not because I have it all figured out — I really, truly don't — but because following Jesus is sweeter when we do it together, even across a screen.

Here you'll find devotional reflections, poetry written in the margins of ordinary days, scripture I'm sitting with, and honest letters about what it means to be a young woman learning to be still in a loud world.

A few things about me

  • I write with a cup of tea that's usually gone cold before I finish it.
  • My Bible is the most underlined book I own, and I love it that way.
  • I post slowly and intentionally — about once a month — because good words take time.
  • I believe the ordinary moments are where God does His most beautiful work.
  • I'm still learning, still growing, still becoming. Just like you.
"Be still, and know that I am God."
— Psalm 46:10  ·  a verse I return to often

If something here has landed in your heart, I would truly love to hear from you. You can reach me through the contact page — I read every message and try to write back to as many as I can.

Thank you for being here. Really.
