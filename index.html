A small psalm for the in-between

A short verse from this month's collection, written in a season of waiting.

I am learning the slow grammar of waiting —
how still is a verb, and hush is a kind of prayer.
How even the lilies, before they bloom,
must spend a long winter in the dark
becoming. — Maggie · 2026
